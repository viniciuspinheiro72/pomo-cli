# Product Brief

## What It Is
A command-line Pomodoro timer that manages focused work sessions and breaks directly from the terminal.

## Who It's For
Developers and knowledge workers who live in the terminal and want distraction-free time management without switching to a GUI app.

## The Problem It Solves
Existing Pomodoro apps require leaving the terminal or installing heavy GUI tools. There's no lightweight, scriptable CLI option that integrates naturally into a developer workflow.

## Key Features
- Start, pause, and stop timed Pomodoro sessions (25 min default)
- Automatic short break (5 min) and long break (15 min after 4 sessions)
- Desktop notification when a session or break ends
- Session history saved locally (JSON)
- Configurable session and break durations

## Business Objective
A well-used personal productivity tool that can be published to npm and adopted by the developer community.

## Out of Scope
- GUI or web interface
- Cloud sync or team features
- Task/todo management (timer only)
- Integrations with external services (Jira, Notion, etc.)
